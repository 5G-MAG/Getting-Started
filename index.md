Today, specifications in media and connectivity technologies are ultimately realised through software implementations. Developing robust software requires specific expertise, tools, and support â€“ resources the **5G-MAG Reference Tools Developer Community** makes available for a structured approach to software development alongside specification work.

With the **5G-MAG Reference Tools** we:
* Accelerate progress through early and continuous feedback from implementations
* Support higher quality specifications thanks to earlier validation in real-world environments
* Foster adoption through the availability of open-source software and tools that lower entry barriers for product development
